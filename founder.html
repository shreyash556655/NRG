Our Founder

The vision and leadership behind NRG Auto

Ravindra Motiram Gaikwad

Mr. Ravindra Motiram Gaikwad is the founder of NRG Auto, driven by a vision to make electric mobility accessible, affordable, and reliable for Indian customers.

With a strong belief in sustainability and innovation, he continues to guide NRG Auto toward a future focused on clean energy and smart transportation.

His leadership emphasizes customer trust, product quality, and long-term environmental responsibility.
